This website is an independent project by volunteers of Open Charlotte to help encourage local voter engagement.

This website has no political affiliation and is not affiliated with the City of Charlotte or Mecklenburg County, or their underlying departments. Content on this website has been collected and shared by residents interested in helping residents learn and partake in their elections. To flag an inaccuracy, submit an "issue" or "pull request" through the site's GitHub page here.
